Tip of the week 5

Any.Do

A fancy, useful, cool app to manage your tasks and improve your productivity ;-). It's a very simple app coming with :

- Android app (with widgets, status bar notifications,)
- Iphone app
- Chrome extension
- HTML5 app : coming soon

Why it's cool ?

- Syncs with all your devices of course (and your google tasks too !)
- Very simple to use
- Missed a call ? replan it !
- Share tasks (ie : shopping list)
- Plan tasks (with an alarm)
- Widgets for your desktop
- and more

So, check this out now on Any.do !
